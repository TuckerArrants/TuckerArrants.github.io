Bio

“I was born not knowing and have had only a little time to change that here and there.” - Richard Feynman

I attended Awty International high school in Houston, TX where I was exposed to many mathematically oriented individuals that steered me in the direction of studying physics in college. During this time, I was introduced to many advanced mathematical techniques and I picked up some coding experience from scientific computing courses (all Python based). Upon graduation, I decided to take my strong background in theoretical math and apply it to the riveting field of data science and AI.

I am currently interning at Pacific Life as a data analyst in HR where I am helping various groups automate their reporting processes. During this pandemic, I have ventured deep into natural language processing and computer vision. I have a couple models that I aim to deploy either in browser or in apps, as soon as I learn the prerequisites
